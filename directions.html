Directions

Driving Directions to Community Catholic Center, 3525 Rudd Avenue.

• If coming from I-64 heading west, take the 22nd Street exit.

• If coming from I-264 west, continue to I-64 east. Take the 22nd Street exit.

Turn right at the first light (the Shell station) which is 22nd Street and Portland Ave. Continue on Portland Ave. until 33rd Street, which is a 5-way intersection at Northwestern Parkway. Turn right onto 33rd Street. Take 33rd Street one block until Rudd Ave. Turn left onto Rudd and continue a few blocks. CCC/Lehmann Hall will be just past Good Shepherd Church on the right.

Street parking is available in front of Lehmann. Parking is also available between the church and Lehmann Hall.

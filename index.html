Community Catholic Center

Educate. Empower. Inspire.


Community Catholic Center Inc., a 501(c) (3) non-profit organization, works in the Portland and West End neighborhood of Louisville, Kentucky, to assist families in providing a Catholic education to their children.

Several Catholic elementary schools and high schools partner with CCC to provide a comprehensive Catholic education to the children in our program.

With generous support from donors, CCC provides financial assistance for tuition at local Catholic schools and enrichment opportunities to further develop the students in the program.

Community Catholic Center is dedicated to making a quality Catholic education available and accessible to children living in a community where no Catholic schools are present. First and foremost CCC works with the family to meet the tuition needs of the students; however, through a holistic model CCC also works to improve the students’ overall well-being by incorporating education and personal development activities.

  • Student Education: Helping students attend a Catholic school is only the first step in CCC’s work with a students. CCC provides tutoring and academic support in addition to representation at school conferences.
  • Student & Family Support: With the goal to give each student the opportunity to achieve his/her full potential, CCC provides an on-staff social worker who facilitates family and student involvement in the schools and in the community. We also provide families with professional representation and collaboration when working with schools and educational services.
  • Student Enrichment: CCC offers enrichment activities that are intended to nurture a child’s self-confidence while expanding life experiences. These activities are meant to stretch students’ imaginations through seeing new possibilities.

Educate

When families come to CCC seeking tuition assistance in sending their children to Catholic schools, CCC works with the family to create a successful partnership. CCC and the parents enter into an annual contract to guide the partnership. Listed below are parent commitments in the contract.

Family Commitment

  • Parents will pay their share of tuition directly to the school. CCC is not responsible for any unpaid tuition, fees, or School Choice Money. Failure to pay tuition/fees will result in being release from CCC’s program.
  • Parents will pay all fees and book bills.
  • Parents will participate in at least one CCC Service Project.
  • Parent will share their talents by volunteering with the 5th Friday Good Shepherd Bingo, the Good Shepherd Festival, and the Portland Festival.
  • Parents must apply for Catholic Education Foundation (CEF) and Angela Mason Scholarships.
  • Parents are encouraged to apply for School Choice Scholarshps. Parents will attend monthly Family Meetings.
  • Parents will participate in school events including parent conferences and meetings.
  • Parents will create a positive learning environment at home by reading to children, checking homework, and studying nightly.

Directions

Driving Directions to Community Catholic Center 3525 Rudd Avenue

  • If coming from I-64 heading west, take the 22nd Street exit.
  • If coming from I-264 west, continue to I-64 east. Take the 22nd Street exit.

Turn right at the first light (the Shell station) which is 22nd Street and Portland Ave.

Continue on Portland Ave. until 33rd Street, a 5-way intersection at Northwestern Parkway.

Turn right onto 33rd Street.

Take 33rd Street one block until Rudd Ave.

Turn left onto Rudd and continue a few blocks.

CCC/Lehmann Hall is immediately past Good Shepherd Church on your right.

Street parking is available in front of Lehmann Hall. Parking is also available between the church and Lehmann.

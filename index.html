Community Catholic Center

Educate. Empower. Inspire.


Community Catholic Center Inc., a 501(c) (3) non-profit organization, works in the Portland and West End neighborhood of Louisville, Kentucky, to assist families in providing a Catholic education to their children.

Several Catholic elementary schools and high schools partner with CCC to provide a comprehensive Catholic education to the children in our program.

With generous support from donors, CCC provides financial assistance for tuition at local Catholic schools and enrichment opportunities to further develop the students in the program.

Volunteer Opportunities

Become a Volunteer

Participate in the following ways:

  • Serve as a Jr. High/High School Mentor
  • Tutor students after school
  • Volunteer as a cook or server for the Christmas Celebration (December) or Achievement Ceremony (May)
  • Organize a school supply or snack drive to provide needed items for student support

If you are interested in volunteering for these opportunities, email your request toHeidi at Community Catholic Centerand information as well as a background check form will be sent to you.

Pray for Our Success– As all things come from God, CCC continually needs prayers to continue its work. Please pray for the CCC students and families as well as for our efforts to educate, empower and inspire their faith and educational development.

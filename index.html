Community Catholic Center

Educate. Empower. Inspire.


Community Catholic Center Inc., a 501(c) (3) non-profit organization, works in the Portland and West End neighborhood of Louisville, Kentucky, to assist families in providing a Catholic education to their children.

Several Catholic elementary schools and high schools partner with CCC to provide a comprehensive Catholic education to the children in our program.

With generous support from donors, CCC provides financial assistance for tuition at local Catholic schools and enrichment opportunities to further develop the students in the program.
